# Hackathon delta

## Pre-existing Understand-Anything capabilities

- Repository scanning and deterministic structural analysis.
- Semantic node summaries and architecture layers.
- Knowledge graph types, validation, persistence, and search.
- Guided learning tours and graph-first dashboard exploration.
- Source explanation and onboarding guide builders.
- Source-file allowlisting in the local viewer.
- Graph freshness and working-tree drift detection.

## New capabilities proposed for this submission period

- A versioned video-story schema and declarative scene vocabulary.
- Deterministic graph slicing and a source-grounded narration evidence pack.
- GPT-5.6 pedagogical storyboard planning with schema-only output.
- Independent narration review with provenance.
- A hard duration compiler using measured speech clips.
- Provider-neutral English speech with fixture, local-adapter, and production
  provider boundaries.
- SRT and WebVTT compilation plus subtitle burn-in.
- A deterministic visual renderer and media muxer.
- Independent A/V, duration, subtitle, grounding, and provenance verification.
- Hash-bound manifests and machine-readable render receipts.
- External TEMPO governance integration without vendoring the framework.

At this planning checkpoint, these are proposed rather than implemented. No
claim of completion should be made until the governed build and its executable
receipts exist.
